# FamMap Family FAQ (常見問題)

## General Questions

### What is FamMap?
**FamMap (親子地圖)** is a free map-based platform helping Taiwan families discover and plan visits to kid-friendly locations. It includes parks, restaurants, nursing rooms, museums, amusement parks, and medical facilities.

### Is FamMap free to use?
Yes! FamMap is completely free. No registration required to explore locations.

### Do I need internet to use FamMap?
- First visit: Yes, you need internet to load the map and location data
- After loading: The app caches data and works offline with limited features
- Best experience: Stable internet connection

### Can I use FamMap on my phone/tablet?
Yes! FamMap is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on:
- iPhones and iPads
- Android phones and tablets
- Computers and laptops
- Any device with a modern web browser

### How do I install FamMap on my phone?
**iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Safari browser
2. Go to FamMap website
3. Tap Share button (↑)
4. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
5. App will appear on your home screen

**Android:**
1. Open Chrome browser
2. Go to FamMap website
3. Tap menu (⋮)
4. Tap "Install app"
5. App will appear on your home screen

## Location & Map Questions

### How do I find my current location?
1. Tap the location icon (🎯) in the top-right
2. Allow location access when asked
3. Map automatically centers on your location

### How do I search for a specific place?
1. Tap the search bar at the top
2. Type location name (e.g., "Taipei Zoo", "公館")
3. Or type category (e.g., "parks", "nursing rooms")
4. Tap result to see details

### Why isn't my favorite place on FamMap?
1. FamMap is community-driven
2. Tap "Add Location" to suggest a new place
3. Include name, address, type, and why it's family-friendly
4. Moderators review and add within 48 hours

### Can I filter locations by category?
Yes! Tap the filter icon or search by category:
- Parks (公園)
- Nursing Rooms (哺乳室)
- Restaurants (餐廳)
- Medical Centers (醫療設施)
- Attractions (景點)

### How is location distance calculated?
Distance is calculated from your current location to:
- Walking distance: Straight line (as crow flies)
- Real travel time: Via roads, public transit
- Use "Get Directions" for turn-by-turn navigation

## Facility & Amenity Questions

### What does the 👶 (nursing room) icon mean?
Nursing rooms have:
- Private/semi-private space
- Seating for nursing mothers
- Changing tables
- Refrigerator for breast milk storage
- WiFi and power outlets (often)
- Clean, comfortable environment

### Can I bring outside food?
Check each location's "Outside Food Policy":
- ✅ Allowed - Bring your own food freely
- ⚠️ Limited - Allowed in designated areas only
- ❌ Not allowed - Must buy on-site
- 📍 Picnic areas available

### Which locations have changing tables?
1. Search any location
2. Look for 👶 symbol (infant facilities)
3. Check "Facilities" section
4. Call ahead to confirm availability

### Are there playgrounds suitable for babies?
1. Search "Parks" category
2. Filter by age: 👶 (babies/newborns)
3. Check "Age Recommendations"
4. Read parent reviews for baby experiences

### Which locations offer high chairs?
1. Search restaurants or attractions
2. Check facilities list
3. Call ahead: "Do you have high chairs available?"
4. Confirm hygiene and secure attachment

## Crowd & Timing Questions

### How do I avoid crowds?
1. Check "Crowd Levels" section
2. View hourly patterns (peak/off-peak)
3. Check "Best Visit Time" recommendations
4. Avoid peak hours (noon-3pm, evenings)
5. Visit on weekdays, not weekends/holidays

### When is the best time to visit?
FamMap shows for each location:
- Quiet hours (usually 9-11am, 3-5pm)
- Peak hours (usually noon-3pm)
- Weekend vs weekday differences
- School holiday crowd forecasts

### How do you calculate crowd levels?
Based on:
- Historical visitor patterns
- Time of day analysis
- Seasonal variations
- School calendar impact
- Public holidays

### Why is a location closed on certain days?
Common reasons:
- Regular maintenance days
- Weekly rest days
- Seasonal closures
- Special event days
- Typhoon/emergency closures

## Weather & Seasonal Questions

### How do I plan for rainy season?
1. Check "Rainy Day Alternatives" section
2. See indoor activity options
3. View covered area percentages (☂️)
4. Check "Weather Protection" information
5. Note venue closure policies during typhoons

### Is summer too hot to visit?
Check "Weather & Sun Safety" section:
- ☀️ UV risk level for each location
- Temperature forecast
- Shade/AC availability
- Best times (early morning, evening)
- Sun protection recommendations

### What about air quality?
Check "Air Quality" section:
- 💨 AQI (Air Quality Index)
- PM2.5 levels
- Respiratory risk for sensitive children
- Indoor vs outdoor options
- Best seasons for outdoor activities

### Which season is best?
Taiwan seasons and FamMap recommendations:
- **Spring (Apr-May)** - 🌸 Mild weather, low UV
- **Summer (June-Aug)** - ☀️ Hot/humid, check indoor alternatives
- **Fall (Sep-Nov)** - 🍂 Comfortable, good for outdoor
- **Winter (Dec-Feb)** - ❄️ Air quality concerns (PM2.5), low UV

## Technical Questions

### Why can't I see the map?
Try these solutions:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache and reload
4. Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
5. Restart your device

### How do I change the language?
1. Tap the language toggle (中/E) in the top menu
2. All content switches to English/Chinese
3. Preference is saved for next visit
4. Works even in offline mode

### My location isn't accurate
1. Check if location services are enabled
2. Go to app settings and enable location access
3. Wait a few seconds for GPS to calibrate
4. Try moving to an outdoor, open area
5. Zoom in on the map manually

### Can I use FamMap offline?
Limited offline features:
- ✅ View cached locations
- ✅ Read location details (if previously loaded)
- ✅ View saved favorites
- ❌ Search for new locations
- ❌ Get real-time directions
- Best offline: Safari, Chrome (download for offline)

## Privacy & Safety Questions

### Is my location data safe?
Yes, FamMap protects your privacy:
- Location data processed locally on your device
- No personal data stored on servers
- All connections encrypted (HTTPS)
- Optional login - works without registration
- You control location sharing (can turn off anytime)

### What data does FamMap collect?
- Minimal data collection
- Can use app anonymously
- No tracking cookies
- Location history not stored
- See "Privacy Policy" for full details

### Can I report incorrect information?
Yes! For each location:
1. Tap the location
2. Scroll to "Report Issue"
3. Select issue type (hours, facilities, etc.)
4. Provide correction details
5. Moderators review and update

### What if I feel unsafe somewhere?
FamMap includes safety information:
- Staff supervision levels
- Safety ratings
- First aid availability
- Emergency contact information
- Report safety concerns to venue directly

## Troubleshooting

### The map won't load
1. Check internet connection
2. Try a different network (WiFi vs mobile data)
3. Clear browser cache: Settings > Privacy
4. Restart your browser
5. Try FamMap on a different device

### I can't find a location I searched for
1. Try different spelling/name
2. Use Chinese or English name
3. Search by address instead
4. Check if it's permanently closed
5. Suggest it as a new location

### Reviews aren't showing
1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Wait for page to fully load
4. Older reviews may be archived
5. Contact support if reviews missing

### My favorites disappeared
1. Check if you're logged in
2. Log out and log in again
3. Clear browser cache and reload
4. Favorites may be device-specific if not logged in
5. Contact support with details
