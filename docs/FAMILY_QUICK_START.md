# FamMap Quick Start Guide for Families

**FamMap (親子地圖)** is your trusted companion for discovering family-friendly locations in Taiwan. This guide helps you get started quickly.

## 🚀 Getting Started (5 minutes)

### 1. **Open FamMap**
- Visit the FamMap website on your phone, tablet, or computer
- The app works offline once loaded!

### 2. **Allow Location Access**
When the app asks: "Can FamMap use your location?"
- Tap **"Allow"** to find nearby family-friendly places
- If you skip this, you can search for locations manually

### 3. **Your First Search**
Choose how to find places:
- **"Find Me"** (location icon) - Shows nearby places
- **Search bar** - Type a location name or category
- **Categories** - Browse Parks, Restaurants, Nursing Rooms, Medical centers

## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Find a Nearby Park for Your Kids
1. Tap the location icon (🎯)
2. Wait for the map to load nearby parks
3. Tap a park marker to see details
4. Read reviews from other parents

### Find a Nursing Room
1. Use the search bar or tap "Nursing Rooms" category
2. See distance and walking time to each location
3. Check facilities: changing tables, WiFi, refrigerators
4. Call ahead to confirm availability

### Plan Your Family Outing
1. Search for multiple locations (parks, restaurants, nursing rooms)
2. Check operating hours (⏰ icon)
3. Review crowding patterns (peak/quiet times)
4. Check age recommendations (👧👦 icons)
5. Save favorites for quick access (♥ icon)

### Save Your Favorite Places
1. Tap the heart (♥) icon on any location
2. Access saved places anytime from the Favorites menu
3. Plan future trips with your saved list

## 📊 Understanding Location Information

### Facility Indicators
- 👶 **Nursing Room** - Dedicated space for nursing mothers
- 🚽 **Bathrooms** - Toilet facilities (note: kid-friendly amenities)
- 🍴 **Food** - Dining options available
- ♿ **Accessible** - Wheelchair accessible
- 🚇 **Transit** - Near public transportation (MRT/bus)

### Crowd Levels
- 🟢 **Quiet** - Best time to visit
- 🟡 **Moderate** - Acceptable crowding
- 🔴 **Peak** - Very busy hours
- Check "Best Visit Time" to avoid crowds

### Age Recommendations
- 👶 Suitable for babies/newborns
- 👧 Good for toddlers (1-3 years)
- 👦 Fun for preschool (3-6 years)
- 🧒 Activities for school-age (6+ years)

## 📞 Need Help?

### Call Ahead
- Phone numbers provided for every location
- Confirm availability before visiting
- Ask about current conditions/maintenance
- Check group booking policies

### Questions to Ask
- "Are you open today?" (Confirm hours)
- "How busy are you right now?" (Check crowd level)
- "Do you have high chairs/changing tables?" (Confirm facilities)
- "Can we bring outside food?" (Check policies)
- "Is there parking?" (Confirm details)
