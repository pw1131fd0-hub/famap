# FamMap Venue Operator Guide

Welcome to the FamMap Venue Manager Portal! This guide helps venue/location operators effectively manage their facility information and engage with families.

## 📋 Getting Started

### What is the Venue Manager Portal?

The Venue Manager Portal is a dedicated interface for location operators to:
- Manage facility information
- Update operating hours and pricing
- Monitor reviews and ratings
- Respond to customer feedback
- Track visitor analytics
- Manage events and classes
- Upload facility photos

### Who Should Use This?

- Park managers
- Restaurant owners
- Nursing room facility staff
- Medical facility administrators
- Attraction managers
- Any location listed on FamMap

## 🔑 Account Setup

### Create a Venue Account

1. Visit FamMap website
2. Select "Venue Operators" or "Venue Login"
3. Click "Register as Venue"
4. Provide business information:
   - Venue name
   - Contact email
   - Physical address
   - Phone number
   - Business registration/license
5. Verify email address
6. Set password
7. Complete venue profile

### Verify Your Venue

1. Log in to Venue Portal
2. Go to "Venue Settings"
3. Add verification documents:
   - Business license
   - Proof of address
   - Manager ID
4. Submit for verification
5. Wait for FamMap team approval (24-48 hours)

## 📍 Manage Location Information

### Update Basic Information

1. Go to "Venue Dashboard"
2. Select your location
3. Update:
   - Venue name and description
   - Address and location
   - Contact phone number
   - Website URL
   - Email address
4. Save changes

### Add/Edit Operating Hours

1. Select "Operating Hours"
2. Set hours for each day:
   - Monday - Sunday
   - Special hours for holidays
   - Seasonal variations
3. Mark as "Closed" if not operating
4. Add special notes (early closing, seasonal closures)
5. Save

### Manage Pricing

1. Go to "Pricing Information"
2. Add entry/usage fees
3. Set pricing by:
   - Age group
   - Entry type (daily, hourly, yearly)
   - Seasonal variations
4. Add discount information:
   - Group discounts
   - Membership benefits
   - Free entry days/hours
5. Save pricing

### Update Facilities

1. Select "Facilities & Amenities"
2. Check/uncheck available facilities:
   - Stroller accessibility
   - High chairs available
   - Changing tables
   - Nursing rooms
   - Bathrooms
   - Parking
   - WiFi
   - Dining options
3. Add facility details:
   - Number of each item
   - Cleanliness notes
   - Maintenance schedule
4. Update facilities as they change

### Add Taiwan-Specific Features

1. Go to "Taiwan Family Features"
2. Fill in available information:
   - **Transit**: MRT/bus stops nearby, accessibility
   - **Parking**: Availability, pricing, accessibility spaces
   - **Dining**: On-site options, dietary restrictions
   - **Medical**: Nearby hospitals, first aid availability
   - **Weather**: Sun protection, rainy day alternatives
   - **Safety**: Security measures, lost child protocols
   - **Amenities**: Water fountains, rest areas, equipment rental
   - **Events**: Classes, performances, special activities
   - **Staff**: Languages spoken, training certifications
3. Update regularly to keep information current
4. Save

## 📸 Upload Photos & Media

### Add Location Photos

1. Go to "Photos & Media"
2. Click "Upload Photos"
3. Select images showing:
   - Overall facility view
   - Play areas/main attractions
   - Facilities (bathrooms, nursing rooms)
   - Dining areas
   - Parking
4. Add captions for each photo
5. Mark best photos as "Featured"
6. Arrange in desired order
7. Save

### Photo Guidelines

- **Quality**: Clear, well-lit photos
- **Accuracy**: Recent photos (not outdated)
- **Variety**: Show different areas
- **Family Focus**: Highlight kid-friendly features
- **Content**: No inappropriate content
- **Permissions**: Ensure you have rights to use photos
- **Privacy**: Don't show identifiable people without permission

## 📊 Monitor Reviews & Ratings

### View Reviews

1. Go to "Reviews & Ratings"
2. See all reviews sorted by:
   - Newest first (default)
   - Highest rated
   - Lowest rated
   - By facility type
3. Filter reviews by:
   - Date range
   - Rating (1-5 stars)
   - Facility type

### Respond to Reviews

**Best Practices:**
- Respond promptly (within 24-48 hours)
- Be professional and courteous
- Address specific feedback
- Offer to fix issues
- Thank positive reviewers
- Invite for return visit

**Example Responses:**

**For Positive Review:**
> Thank you for visiting and for this wonderful review! We're delighted you had a great experience. We look forward to seeing your family again!

**For Constructive Feedback:**
> Thank you for the feedback about our facilities. We take cleanliness seriously and have already addressed this. We'd love another chance to provide a better experience!

**For Negative Review:**
> We're sorry to hear about your experience. This doesn't reflect our standards. Please contact us directly so we can make this right. [Include contact info]

### Request Review Removal

If a review:
- Contains false information
- Is offensive or inappropriate
- Violates community guidelines

1. Click "Report Review"
2. Select reason
3. Provide explanation
4. Submit for review
5. FamMap team will investigate

## 📈 Track Analytics

### View Visitor Analytics

1. Go to "Analytics Dashboard"
2. See key metrics:
   - Page views (location visits)
   - Search appearances
   - Review reads
   - Click-through rates
   - Visitor trends

### Understand the Metrics

**Page Views**: Number of times your location was visited/viewed
**Search Appearances**: How often found in search results
**Review Reads**: People reading your reviews
**Engagement**: Interaction with your facility info
**Trends**: Changes over time (weekly/monthly)

### Use Analytics to Improve

- High page views but low visitor engagement? Improve your photos and description
- Many searches but few pages views? Improve your search ranking with better keywords
- Good views but few reviews? Ask visitors to leave feedback
- Low performance? Update information and add photos

## 🎯 Manage Events & Classes

### Create an Event

1. Go to "Events & Classes"
2. Click "Create Event"
3. Fill in event details:
   - Event name
   - Date and time
   - Duration
   - Age group (min-max)
   - Type (class, workshop, performance, activity)
   - Capacity
   - Cost
   - Description
   - Photos
   - Registration link (if applicable)
4. Add in both Chinese and English
5. Publish

### Event Management

1. View scheduled events
2. Edit upcoming events
3. Mark as "Full" when capacity reached
4. Cancel if needed (with explanation)
5. Archive past events
6. Track event attendance

### Promote Events

- Features in location details
- Appears in event search
- Sent to followers
- Shared in recommendations
- Visible on location page

## 💬 Respond to Questions

### Answer Visitor Questions

1. Go to "Questions & Inquiries"
2. See questions from visitors:
   - About facilities
   - Operating hours
   - Pricing
   - Accessibility
3. Click to respond
4. Provide helpful answers
5. Questions appear in location details

### Common Questions to Prepare For

- What are your operating hours?
- Do you have stroller access?
- Is there on-site parking?
- Do you allow outside food?
- Are there bathrooms available?
- Can we book a group visit?
- Is WiFi available?
- Are there nursing rooms?

## 🆘 Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check email address
- Request password reset
- Verify account is approved

**Photos not showing?**
- Check file size (< 5MB)
- Use common formats (JPG, PNG)
- Verify photo quality
- Check upload completion

**Reviews missing?**
- Reviews appear after moderation (usually instant)
- Check if marked as "spam" accidentally
- Contact support if still missing

**Metrics not updating?**
- Allow 24 hours for sync
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact support

### Contact Support

1. Go to "Help & Support"
2. Select issue category
3. Describe problem with details
4. Attach screenshots if helpful
5. Submit ticket
6. Receive response within 24 hours

## 🔐 Security & Privacy

### Keep Your Account Safe

- Use strong password (12+ characters, mixed case, numbers)
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Don't share login credentials
- Log out on shared computers
- Change password regularly (quarterly)
- Report suspicious activity

### Protect Visitor Privacy

- Don't publish visitor names (unless they agree)
- Remove identifiable info from photos
- Respect privacy in venue photos
- Don't share visitor contact info
- Comply with data protection regulations
